Highland Industries

I worked with Highland Industries, Inc (a textiles manufacturing plant) during a three-rotation co-op starting in January 2020 and ending July 2021. During this time I worked in the Continuous Improvement department focusing on automating tedious tasks and analyzing data produced by the machines.

Below are a sampling of the projects I completed.

Data Analysis and Automation

I built a pipeline for a report that required many hours of data analysis a week. My pipeline completed the analysis with higher accuracy in a few minutes.

Machine Data Analysis and Presentation

At the request of a manager, I worked with an outside contractor to pull run data from one of the textile machines and present it to the floor workers in real time. To accomplish this task, I had to transform and clean the data, display it on a graph with a few shifts work of historical data for a comparison, and update it every minute.

Factory Process Tracking

I designed and built an app to guide floor workers through a specific procedure. The app keeps track of users, times, steps, and any notes or inputs as required for the process. This replaced a paper tracking system and was used throughout the factory.

IT Training

During my first rotation, the IT manager left for another position. In the two weeks before he left, I shadowed him and recorded all aspects of his job to compile a training manual and assist in the onboarding of a new IT manager.
